THE SUSTAINABLE CHOICE

Choosing to renovate or retrofit your home is one of the most sustainable decisions you will ever make

In a world that encourages endless consumption, making the most of what we have is a far better option. Recycle, reuse, retrofit, renew. With targeted upgrades we can dramatically increase the performance of your home, whilst retaining all the character and charm of your existing home.

Without a doubt, the lowest carbon building is an existing one.

WHAT IS HOME PERFORMANCE?

There's much more to a better performing home than just energy efficiency...

The core function of a house is to provide shelter. Most houses do this well, but there's certainly more to it than that. Here are the four pillars of home performance that Castle Calibre strives for:  

healthy

Healthy homes prioritise thermal comfort and fresh air all year round.

affordable

Keeping your home healthy and comfortable shouldn't be a luxury.

responsible

Responsible choices and renewable energy minimise our impact on the earth.

resilient

Resilient homes withstand climate extremes (and rising energy prices).

the Key elements

What elements of a home most impact performance?

In the era of social media, we're all very aware of the current architectural and interior design trends... but if we set aside the (often unrealistic) vision of 'a beautiful home', what are the key elements of a home that can impact our quality of life? 

1
Thermal Envelope

The all-important barrier between the inside of your home and the elements outside.

2
Energy Efficiency

Energy is valuable, so let's put modern technology to work to ensure we make the most of what we use.

3
All-Electric

The term 'natural gas' is a misnomer; it's a fossil fuel, and the planet would really like us to stop burning it.

4
Renewable Energy

Fully realise the potential for an all-electric home by producing your own electricity.

5
Indoor Air Quality

The often overlooked ingredient in home performance. Cleaner, drier, fresher air promotes good health.

6
Certified Value

Add value to your home by making its performance official. Have your home assessed by a government-accredited expert.

To best improve these elements and maximise the value of your investment, consider a home energy assessment. With my expert knowledge, you can simplify your home performance journey and ensure maximum results.
