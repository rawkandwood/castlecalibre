Residential Efficiency Scorecard

An innovative, government-backed program that assesses homes for energy efficiency and comfort. The assessment outcomes include an official certificate and a star rating for your home, with detailed advice on how to improve this rating and increase your home's liveability.

Home Energy Handbook

In addition to your Scorecard Certificate, I provide you with a handbook containing tailored recommendations, a resource guide and a deeper dive into home energy performance topics. Written specifically for your home and your situation, this document will serve as a valuable resource for your home liveability journey.
